Cinnamon Rolls

Igredients

  • Milk
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon

Recipe

  1. Mix yeast, a bit of milk, flour and sugar in a bowl. Then let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix the rest of the igredients (except cinnamon) and add leaven. Then let it sit for an hour.
  3. Add cinnamon, make rolls and let them sit for 30 minutes.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes.
